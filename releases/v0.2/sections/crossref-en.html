Dataset Linkage ID Mappingc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/shp#DatasetLinkageIdMapping

Linkage file maps the original identifiable ID (e.g. such as CHI) in the respective dataset to a project and dataset specific new UID. In other words, those UIDs are different for each dataset in a project. This is to prevent premature linkage of the data before is has been fully anonymised.
has super-classes
TRE Project Document c

Research Coordinatorc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/shp#ResearchCoordinator

In the Grampian Data Safe Haven, Research Coordinators facilitate the use of routinely collected health and population data for research. Research Coordinators also ensure all information governance and information security measures related to research projects, including reviewing project permissions, approved researcher access periods, appropriate training has been undertaken before and during access to the TRE. Research Coordinators support researchers from academic, NHS and industry to design, plan, gather permissions and conduct research involving health and administrative data.
has super-classes
person

Researcherc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/shp#Researcher

A person who carries out academic, scientific or clinical research. Researchers who require a TRE (or SDE/Safe Haven), must have any research project approved by ethical permissions to undertake the research and require enhanced information governance and security training to become approved to access highly-sensitive data within the TRE.
has super-classes
person

Sign Offc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/shp#SignOff

Senior member of staff reviewed the validated datasets for data integrity and data pseudonymisation. Data signoff has more of an emphasis on validity of extraction, data validation process and aligns with research project ethical permissions. This is a key step prior to data transferring between different servers/environments (e.g. NHS data moving onto University servers, or data on University servers being released to a project folder for a researcher to access.
has super-classes
Data Check c

Documents that bare some form of importance and information required for the delivery of the TRE project.
